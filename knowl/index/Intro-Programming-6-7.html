The other type of process that we have is a subroutine. This is a command that runs a more complex piece of code. This is where most of the hard work of programming is done, which also means that this is the part we’re going to gloss over the most in this class. For the things we do, we’ll use descriptive names for our processes so that you can have an intuitive sense of what’s happening without needing to go through the details.
